ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Ndenuka pre & primary school is a registered learning institution committed to providing quality, safe, and value-based education for pupils. The school operates under the registration number EM 17703, in compliance with educational standards and regulations.

with a team of qualified and caring teachers, a child friendly learning environment, and a strong partnership with parents and community Ndenuka pre & primary school strives to prepare learners with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to succeed in school and in life.

Our Values

At Ndenuka primary school, our values guide everything we do. They shape our culture, our teaching, and the way we prepare learners for life beyond the classroom.

Integrity

We believe in honesty, accountability and doing what is right at all time

Excellence

We strive for excellence in academics, creativity, and personal growth

Innovation & Creativity

We encourage curiosity, critical thinking, and creative problem solving

Care & Compassion

We believe education must be rooted in love, care and understanding

leadership begins with self-discipline and a willingness to serve others.

Respect

We value every individual and celebrate diversity

Safety First

We priotize the physical, emotional, and digital safety of all learners at all time.

Responsibility & Leadership

We prepare learners to become responsible individual and future leaders.
